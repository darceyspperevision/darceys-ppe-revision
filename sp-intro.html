Introducing Social Policy and the Welfare State

Reading List

Topic 1: Introducing Social Policy and the Welfare State

In this first session, we introduce a range of debates about the contested nature and purposes of social policy. Debates about the goals and possibilities of social policy are discussed, with particular reference to different ideological perspectives on welfare, the theories underlying the initial development of welfare states, and the relationship between the welfare state and citizenship, in particular social citizenship.

Sample Questions

  1. Discuss different views about the goals or purposes of social policy and possible reasons for them.
  2. What factors drove the emergence of the welfare state in the UK?
  3. What is social citizenship? To what extent did ideas about social citizenship matter for the development of modern welfare states?
