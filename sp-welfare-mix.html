Reading List

Topic 3: The Changing Nature of the Welfare Mix

This session introduces debates on the welfare mix. It discusses ways in which the role of the state vis-à-vis the market and voluntary sectors and the family/informal care has changed over time in the UK in terms of benefits and services; why; and with what implications. We also look briefly at the ‘social divisions of welfare’ – not only public, but also fiscal and occupational.

Sample Questions

  1. Does the privatisation of finance, provision and regulation have different effects on welfare outcomes?
  2. Is public and state-based provision of welfare superior to private and market-based forms of provision?
  3. Have recent changes in the welfare mix in the UK resulted more from concerns about efficiency than from concerns about equality.
