Worlds of Welfare

Reading List

Topic 2: Worlds of Welfare

Approaches to welfare differ not only over time but also across national contexts. This session examines ways to conceptualize cross-national differences through typologies of welfare regimes (ways to group welfare states in ‘regimes’), including the fundamental feminist critique to mainstream notions and explanations of the origins of different welfare arrangements.

Sample Questions

  1. How typical is the UK today of a liberal welfare regime?
  2. Feminist perspectives have profoundly altered conceptions of welfare regimes. Discuss.
  3. Does the literature on welfare regimes pay too much attention to cash transfer programs and not enough to services?
