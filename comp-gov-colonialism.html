Colonialism

This topic examines the relationship between colonial rule and various outcomes, such as democracy and economic well-being. We examine both the economic and political dimensions of colonial legacy from a comparative perspective, exploring different structures and traditions. We investigate conditions that determined the variation in the long-term consequences of colonial rule and ask why some colonial systems left a stronger imprint than others.

Essay Questions

  1. What effects did colonial occupation have on the trajectory of democracy in colonised countries?
  2. How did different colonial institutions affect economic and political outcomes in former colonies?
