Class, Education and Economic Inequalities

Reading List

Topic 3: Class, Education and Economic Inequalities

Sample Questions

  1. Is class politics a result of self-interest or socialisation? (PPE 2020)
  2. Why has the pattern of class voting in Western countries changed in recent decades?
  3. How do class, education, wealth and other economic inequalities affect voting behaviour?

Readings
